Here is the basic student path in TeachPlay. Everything starts from the same learner workspace.
If this is for real course credit, students should sign in first so progress and certificate records can be saved.
From the credential card, students review the expectations, then enter the guided course.
Inside the course, the 12 modules are the main learning path. The portfolio checkpoints are where students collect evidence for review.
Students work through the lessons, use the case studies, try the beginner AI prompts, and mark progress as they go.
When the portfolio is ready, students open the final submission, add their context and evidence, and send it for review.
After completion, the certificate handoff shows what was earned and where the downloadable credential evidence lives.
