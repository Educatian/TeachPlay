How Hashing Works

Three reasons to hash locally.

Privacy

Files never upload

SHA-256 runs in your browser via crypto.subtle.digest. The handbook sees only the hex digest — never the bytes.

Integrity

Evidence becomes verifiable

The hash anchors each artifact to your assertion. A facilitator who re-hashes the file you send can confirm it's the same one you submitted.

Portability

Move the hash, not the file

Pin the artifact wherever you like — Drive, LMS, personal site. The credential refers to the content, not its location.
