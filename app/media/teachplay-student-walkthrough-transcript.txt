TeachPlay Student Walkthrough

Welcome to TeachPlay, the University of Alabama microcredential for AI-enhanced educational game design.

Students begin in the catalog, where the microcredential is presented with the time commitment, CEU value, and evidence expectations.

Open the credential page to review what you will produce: a design blueprint, playable prototype, AI provenance log, playtest report, and revision rationale.

The access guide summarizes the full learner path: start from the catalog, enter the guided course, build evidence, and submit for review.

When you enter the guided course, the curriculum sidebar shows modules, lesson progress, bookmarks, and discussion access in one workspace.

Work through each lesson, mark it complete, and continue. Your progress updates as you move through the credential sequence.

When the evidence packet is ready, submit it for instructor review. Feedback and credential status are managed through the platform workflow.
