TeachPlay Avatar Mini-Lecture: AI Provenance and Human Judgment

In TeachPlay, AI is not magic dust. It is a design partner, and its work needs to be documented.

A strong provenance log tells us why you used AI, what you asked for, what it produced, and what you changed.

The key question is simple: can a reviewer still see your human judgment?

In Chalk and Chance, the AI students create practice conditions, but the teacher decision remains the real learning target.

For your own prototype, write one clear AI role card. What can the AI do, what can it not do, and how will you review it?

Make the path visible: prompt, output, edit, and final design decision.

That turns AI use from a black box into evidence of responsible instructional design.
